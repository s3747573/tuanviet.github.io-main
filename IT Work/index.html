IT Professionals

Here are the IT professionals to summarize and discuss about:

Zach from I. T. Career Questions

Video Link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=om8AygYdrto

He works for a hospital as an Information Systems Technician where he helps support close to 2000 people and over 1000 devices. On a daily basis, although it can vary Zach would naturally check the ticketing system of the hospital, answering phone calls, solving problems in his department, creating user accounts for patients. He spends most of his time in the IT office where the computer set-up is and on the computer he uses many tools to help manage and store data. One of the common challenge in IT is the job will always be different, ‘putting out fire’ and rarely get to do what you desperately want to.

Utsavized from Microsoft

Video link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lcw-MkO38sA

Utsav is a senior software engineer at Microsoft. The company can still operate 100% remotely for engineering therefore quarantine has not impacted his work flow since he mostly works from home anyway. His day would start with a standup which consists of checking emails, update tasks, status and review meeting agendas, goals with co-workers. After that, most of his work revolve around reviewing codes and working on some personal coding.

East Charmer

Video link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DGLagx-d6zA

East Charmer is a Filipina youtuber who works as an IT Support Specialist. Similar to other IT professionals, first things she does in her working hours are checking emails, slack messages and system alerts and have a daily team meeting on Google Meet afterward. When working from home, her problems are feeling distracted, unmotivated, lack of communication between co-workers and physical interactions.

Chris from Datacom Systems in Wellington

Video link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RYdmWa-i7Gs

Chris Sheehan is an IT Support Technician at Datacom Systems. Every day, Chris would answer support queries opened by their customers, updating new software, app releases, website releases. To be efficient as an IT support, one needs to know a bit of everything from software development to service support and networking issues. Chris job requires him to stay at his desk; However, not 100% of the time due to the fact that he does work from home.

DC at Data-Sec, Brisbane, Queensland

Video link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuyVElFHjy8

DC is a cyber-security director at Data-Sec, he is also the co-founder of Airdeskr. Like the other, his working days start with a team meeting, then check tickets and reply to email, inspect logs. Redundancy testing at offsite data center and checking high risk log entries sent by SOC team to find vulnerability and resolve. Finally, DC ends the day by writing reports about the day.
